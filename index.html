Nicasia Beebe-Wang

About

I am a second year PhD student in the Paul G. Allen School for Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by Su-In Lee. My research is focused on developing and applying machine learning techniques for biological problems. My current project aims to identify a molecular basis for Alzheimer's Disease using multi-task deep learning. Before this, I received my BA in Computer Science (with a minor in Statistics) from Harvard University in 2017.
